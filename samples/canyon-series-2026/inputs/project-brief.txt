PROJECT BRIEF — Canyon Series 2026
===================================

Title:      Canyon Series 2026
Platform:   Instagram Portrait (1080 × 1350 px)
Tone:       Editorial
Series:     4 frames

SUBJECT
-------
Canyon landscape photography from the American Southwest. The series
explores how light and shadow reveal geological time — layered rock,
eroded channels, and ridgeline geometry at the edge of day.

NARRATIVE ARC
-------------
Frame 1 (editorial-anchor): Wide opening frame establishes the series
  identity with strong editorial typography over a gradient overlay.
  "Canyon Series" headline anchors the bottom-left zone.

Frame 2 (full-bleed, silent): The photograph speaks alone. No text, no
  overlay. Lets eroded rock channels breathe without interruption.
  Silence map position: [2].

Frame 3 (data-callout): A bold geological stat — "300M years of
  geological history" — positions the series in deep time. Solid overlay
  at 45% opacity. Accent color headline (#B85530).

Frame 4 (minimal-strip): Closing frame with a single poetic line over a
  gradient. "Where light becomes architecture." Cormorant Garamond,
  bottom-center.

DESIGN TOKENS
-------------
Background:  #1a1a2e  (deep navy)
Primary:     #E0D8CE  (warm stone)
Accent:      #B85530  (burnt sienna)
Neutral:     #5C6B74  (slate grey)

Display font:  Cormorant Garamond 700
Body font:     Inter 400 / 700

VARIETY CONTRACT
----------------
zone_max_usage_pct:   40%
shape_quota:          1 shape per 3 frames (no waiver)
overlay_strategies:   gradient + solid-bar (min 2)
accent_color_frames:  [frame-03]
